India’s SS Houseware Exports

  • Till the early eighties, kitchenware in the average Indian home would primarily consist of copper, aluminum, and stainless steel utensils; cutleries like tea-cups, saucers, and spoons; cookware of aluminum; and plastic bottles to store spices, sugar, salt, and edible oil. The only sophisticated cookware was perhaps the pressure-cooker. Gradual changes were seen with stainless steel utensils replacing copper and aluminum.
  • According to the research report, the kitchenware products market will grow at a considerable CAGR rate to reach over INR 55 billion by 2020 due to the increasing number of nuclear families and exposure to online distribution channels.
  • Future growth of the Kitchenware Products Market is expected to be driven by the emergence of web aggregators and the rise in preferences towards branded kitchenware products.
  • “The manufacturing base of the country is expected to expand in the coming future with the government initiative of promoting India as a manufacturing hub, improving technological know-how, and surging number of Point of Sales both online and offline.” according to the Research Analyst, Ken Research.
  • In terms of retail formats, exclusive distributorship is on the decline and sales through large general merchandisers are increasing because of the western mall shopping culture in urban areas. There is a need for more specialized stores dealing in kitchenware products. These can be in the form of large format ‘category-killers’ or an up-market small-size store that deals in specific types of kitchenware products.
  • Total exports of Stainless Steel Utensils from India were USD 324.1 Million in FY 2013-14, which increased to USD 380.04 Million in FY 2014-15. This was an increase of 11.1%. (Source: DGCI&S)

Company Overview

  • The company is a manufacturer of Stainless Steel Houseware. The company manufactures and exports Stainless Steel Utensils, Cutlery, Kitchenware, and Household Articles directly or through Merchant Export Trading Companies.
  • The company has a wide range and ability to produce around 200 different products backed by in-house designing. The company is capable of producing steel houseware of any shape like round, oval, square, or any other shape.
  • All processes, including sheet cutting, mould making, pressing, spinning, welding, polishing, and packing, are done under one roof.
  • A highly skilled labour force ensures that the company has happy and satisfied customers in E.U, U.S.A, U.K., South America, Japan, and many other countries.
  • The company’s stringent quality control measures, high packing standards, and precise time management have enabled it to have tie-ups with many store chains and importer-distributors across geographies. It has also helped in retaining customers.
