About Us

We are a leading manufacturer and exporter of Stainless Steel Houseware, including utensils, cutlery, kitchenware, and household items. Our state-of-the-art facilities in Kundli Industrial Area, near Delhi, are powered by decades of industry expertise and a deep understanding of production processes.

With over 200 customizable products in various shapes like round, oval, square, and more, we manage all production steps in-house — from sheet cutting to packaging. Our skilled workforce helps us maintain a strong global presence across the EU, USA, UK, South America, Japan, and beyond.
