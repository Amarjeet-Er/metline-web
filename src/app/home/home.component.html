About Us

The Company is a manufacturer of Stainless Steel Houseware. The Company manufactures and exports Stainless Steel Utensils, Cutlery, Kitchenware and Household Articles directly or through Merchant Export Trading Companies. The promoters of Metline Houseware. have a cumulative work-experience of many decades in the stainless steel industry. Their acquaintance with all procedures involved in production has been set in motion at the state of the art two manufacturing facilities, both in Kundli Industrial Area on the outskirts of Delhi

The Company has a wide range and ability to produce around 200 different products backed by in-house designing The Company is capable of producing steel houseware of any shape like round, oval, square or any other shape. All processes, sheet cutting mould making, pressing, spinning, welding, polishing and packing are done under one roof. A highly skilled labour force ensures that the Company has happy and satisfied customers in E.U, U.S.A, U.K., South America, Japan and many other countries.

Why Choose Us

We are a trusted manufacturer and exporter of high-quality stainless steel houseware products, including utensils, cutlery, kitchenware, and other household items. Our manufacturing facility is strategically located in the Kundli Industrial Area near Delhi, enabling efficient logistics and timely delivery to both domestic and international markets.

With years of industry experience and an in-depth understanding of global standards, we offer over 200 customizable products in a variety of shapes such as round, oval, square, and more. Our in-house manufacturing process covers every stage — from sheet cutting and shaping to polishing, packing, and final inspection — ensuring consistent quality and cost control.

Our commitment to innovation, durability, and customer satisfaction has helped us build a strong global presence across Europe, the United States, the United Kingdom, South America, Japan, and other international markets. We also take pride in our skilled workforce, advanced equipment, and flexible production capacity, which enable us to handle both bulk orders and custom specifications efficiently.
